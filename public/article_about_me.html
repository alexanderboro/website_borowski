Welcome - Bom freaking dia!

by
Alexander Borowski

From the dynamic streets of Berlin to the sun-kissed landscape of Santos, Lisbon. Join me, as I embark on an exciting new journey of self-expression through this blog. Stay tuned for a tantalizing sneak-peek into my life, spiced with our adventures, and the lessons learned.

A Change of Scenery, A New Journey

After many years in Berlin's bustling startup scene, my long-time friend Max and I decided to make a radical change. We packed our bags, boarded a plane, and found ourselves in Santos, a charming neighborhood in Lisbon. It's here that we began our new venture, but more on that in a seperate post!

The Promise of Authentic Storytelling

I'm excited to welcome you to my blog. Here, you'll find not just the polished highlights, but also the raw, unfiltered experiences. From stories of sun-soaked beaches to navigating the nuances of Lisbon's startup scene, I invite you to join me on this journey of exploration.

Heads Up! Blog Still Under Wraps

Alright, peepz - bear with me here. This blog is still getting its final touches. In the next few weeks, I'll be busy hammering away at some cool new features, chucking in some self-made sketches, and a few other surprises. So hang tight, and watch this space!

Um ótimo dia e tudo de bom!

That's Portuguese for 'Have a great day and all the best'! Keep your eyes peeled for more stories, more updates, and more of life in Lisbon. See you around!
