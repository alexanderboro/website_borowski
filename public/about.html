about me

Hello! I'm Alexander Borowski, 24 years old, originally from Essen. I've had quite a journey - I attended school in New Zealand and then moved to Bonn. These days, I'm studying at CODE University in Berlin. Outside of school, I love seeking out adventures, whether that's rafting, skydiving, surfing, climbing, or running.

the depths of nothing

This blog is my space to share my thoughts and experiences with the world. Why did I call it "the depths of nothing"? Well, life's full of big, unanswered questions. What's the meaning of it all? Through this blog, I hope to explore these questions and more. Stay tuned for more musings from my corner of the world!
