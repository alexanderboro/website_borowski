about me

I'm Alex, 23, living in Berlin at the moment. I'm fascinated by arts, the ocean, digital products, techno, good coffee, philosophies of life in different cultures, the depths of the universe, and so much more. Not only that, but I feel like I need several lifetimes to pursue all my interests.

publishing my thoughts

I'm writing down my thoughts on the internet because perhaps I will reach someone with similar thoughts flying through his head.

the depths of nothing

The bottom line is that we all know nothing. What matters in my life - I'm trying to figure out for myself. Whether I show up to work, improve someone's marketing or hang out in Berghain for another session, it doesn't change anything. We have built societies, norms, and heuristics to simplify life, exclude uncertainties and make it possible to coexist. Ultimately, we all pursue things without looking at the deep meaning of life. For most of us in the western world, it's true: We have stopped wondering. We have stopped questioning.
